# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation respectful and inclusive.

## Our Standards
Be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing standards.

## Scope
This applies within project spaces and public interactions.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans may be applied.
